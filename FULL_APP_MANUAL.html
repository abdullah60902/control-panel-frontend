This system is designed to manage the entire lifecycle of a care home, from admitting a resident to managing the staff who care for them.

The Core Workflow

The system connects three main pillars:

  1. Residents: The people you care for.
  2. Staff (HR): The people providing the care.
  3. Operations: The building, stock, and compliance.
RESIDENT ADMITTED -> CARE PLAN CREATED -> MEDICATION SCHEDULED | | | v v v STAFF ASSIGNED -> DAILY LOGS RECORDED -> COMPLIANCE AUDITED

üìò Part 3: Detailed Module Guide

1. Resident Management

Purpose: To store digital care files.

  • Admitting: Click "Add Resident", fill in name, DOB, and room number.
  • Risk Assessments: Upload documents defining fall risks or dietary needs.

2. Incident Reports

Purpose: Legal documentation of accidents.

The system features a 6-Month Overview chart to track trends (e.g., "Are falls increasing?"). Reports can be exported to PDF for CQC inspections.

3. Compliance & Audits

Purpose: To ensure the home meets regulatory standards.

  • Action Required Filter: Instantly shows expired checks (e.g., "Fire Alarm Test Overdue").
  • Evidence: You can upload photos of safety certificates directly to the record.

üöÄ Summary & Next Steps

This system acts as a "Closed Loop". Every action feeds into a report.

  • If you give a pill -> Stock drops.
  • If you verify a training cert -> Compliance score goes up.
  • If you log an incident -> Analytics dashboard updates immediately.
